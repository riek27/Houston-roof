About Oliver Roof

Building Trust,

Oliver Roof has been the cornerstone of Austin's roofing industry for over 15 years. Founded on the principles of integrity, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction, we've grown from a small local operation to Austin's most trusted roofing company.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our roofing services.

The cost of a new roof in Austin, TX typically ranges from $8,000 to $25,000 depending on several factors including the size of your home, the roofing materials chosen (architectural shingles, metal, tile, etc.), the complexity of the roof design, and any additional work needed such as decking repair or gutter replacement. Oliver Roof provides free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no hidden costs or surprises.

Most residential roof replacements in Austin are completed within 1-3 days for an average-sized home. Larger homes or more complex roofing systems may take up to 5 days. We always provide a clear timeline before beginning work and keep you informed throughout the entire process. Weather is the main factor that can affect timing, but we'll communicate any changes promptly.

Yes! Oliver Roof offers completely free roof inspections and estimates throughout the entire Austin metro area. Our certified inspectors will thoroughly evaluate your roof's condition, check for damage, assess the remaining lifespan, and provide a detailed report with photo documentation — all at absolutely no cost or obligation to you.

For Austin's unique climate, we typically recommend architectural shingles for their excellent durability, heat resistance, and value. Metal roofing is another outstanding option for longevity and energy efficiency, especially for modern homes. For Mediterranean and Spanish-style homes, clay tile roofing provides both beauty and exceptional performance. We'll help you choose the best material based on your home's style, budget, and specific needs.

Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can guide you through the entire claims process. Our team will document all damage, meet with your insurance adjuster, and ensure you receive fair compensation for the repairs needed. We make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible so you can focus on getting your home protected.

We offer a comprehensive workmanship warranty on all our roofing projects, in addition to the manufacturer's warranty on materials. Depending on the roofing system installed, manufacturer warranties can range from 25 years to lifetime coverage. As a GAF Master Elite® contractor, we can offer enhanced warranties that cover both materials and labor. We'll explain all warranty options during your consultation.
